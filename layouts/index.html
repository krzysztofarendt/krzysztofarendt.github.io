Krzysztof Arendt, PhD

I build AI that has to meet engineering reality: physical models, sensor data, constrained devices, simulation tools, and the workflows engineers actually use. The work spans the full modeling spectrum — from high-fidelity numerical simulation and reduced-order physical models to production machine learning.

Some of it is public: peer-reviewed work on simulation and modeling, US patents, and open-source projects in simulation and optimization.

I've worked across academia and industry — years of university research before moving into industry R&D and engineering leadership. That path — from CFD and building-physics research through embedded audio AI to multimodal text-and-vision and agentic workflows — is on the work page.


Areas of focus

  • AI tooling for engineering workflows — applied AI, LLMs and agents, and automation around model validation and engineering work.
  • Applied ML from research to product — taking machine learning from research into industrial and consumer products.
  • Modeling and simulation of technical systems — physics-based modeling, CFD/FEM, Modelica/FMU, EnergyPlus, and white-/gray-/black-box approaches.

Right now I'm at PassiveLogic, working at the intersection of AI, simulation, and sensor-data monitoring and analysis.
