I work at the intersection of AI, simulation, and software engineering for
technical systems. My focus is building AI that has to meet engineering
reality: physical models, sensor data, constrained devices, simulation tools,
and the workflows engineers actually use.

The background runs the full modeling spectrum — from high-fidelity numerical
simulation and reduced-order physical models to production machine learning.
Some of that work is public: peer-reviewed [publications](/publications/) on
AI, simulation, and modeling, US [patents](/publications/), and open-source
[projects](/projects/) in simulation and optimization.

Across these roles I've worked as a hands-on engineer and as a technical
leader, leading AI and engineering teams while staying close to research,
system design, and implementation. The full path — from CFD and building
physics research to embedded audio AI — is on the [work](/work/) page.

## Areas of focus

- **AI tooling for engineering workflows** &mdash; applied AI, LLMs and agents,
  and automation around model validation and engineering work.
- **Applied ML from research to product** &mdash; taking machine learning from
  research into industrial and consumer products.
- **Modeling and simulation of technical systems** &mdash; physics-based
  modeling, CFD/FEM, Modelica/FMU, EnergyPlus, and white-/gray-/black-box
  approaches.

Right now I'm at [PassiveLogic](/work/), working at the intersection of AI,
simulation, and sensor-data monitoring and analysis.
